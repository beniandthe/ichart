## Demo Intent

SM-002 should feel like a real wedding/corporate gig problem:

- the set list is moving quickly,
- the singer changes a key,
- the musician needs a clean chart now,
- the chart should not have to be rewritten from scratch later.

The proof is simple: write a clean chord chart by hand, duplicate it, transpose it to the singer's key, export/share the PDF, and keep the chart for future edits.

## Claim Guardrails

Do not imply:

- iChart imports from another chord-chart app.
- iChart plays backing tracks.
- iChart listens to a song and creates the chart.
- iChart cleans up messy paper charts.
- iChart writes full arrangements or horn parts.

Use:

- `quick clean chord charts`
- `transpose without rewriting`
- `export PDFs`
- `keep the chart for the next gig`
