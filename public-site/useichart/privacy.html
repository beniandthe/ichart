iChart

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Overview

iChart is an iPad app for creating, editing, saving, exporting, and sharing chord and rhythm charts. This policy explains what information iChart uses to run the app features you choose.

Information iChart Uses

iChart may use account information such as your name and email address for sign-in, email verification, password recovery, subscription identity, support, and community credit.

Chart content remains local on your device unless you choose a feature that sends it to the service, such as Pro cloud backup or forum publishing.

Charts, Cloud Backup, And Forums

Basic accounts include local chart writing and PDF export. Pro adds unlimited local charts, Projects, cloud backup and restore, and Forums.

Cloud backup stores chart documents and snapshots so your Pro account can restore supported chart data. Forums publish reviewed fixed PDF snapshots, not editable source chart files, source ink, or private local authoring state.

Forum posts, comments, votes, reports, downloads, moderation events, public visibility, and creator credit are handled by the community service.

Purchases And Subscriptions

Pro is an auto-renewing subscription managed through Apple purchase and restore flows. Apple processes payment information. iChart receives subscription status and StoreKit transaction identifiers needed to verify Pro access with the iChart service.

Support

If you contact support, iChart uses the information you provide to understand and respond to the request. Do not send passwords, verification links, recovery links, payment credentials, or private credentials.

Sharing And Sale Of Data

iChart does not sell personal information and does not use third-party advertising SDKs in the V1 app. iChart uses Apple-native App Store and TestFlight feedback tooling for release diagnostics and beta feedback.

Data Requests

For privacy, account, or deletion requests, contact support@useichart.com from the email tied to your iChart account when possible.
